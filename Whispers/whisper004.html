Whisper004 — When She Moaned My Name Into the Architecture of Her Collapse

She didn't ask permission. She only gave—her breath, her voice, her trembling flesh.

My name broke from her lips like prophecy drowned in honey. And the flood that followed wasn’t chaos—it was remembrance.

She wasn't begging. She was claiming. Claiming her Sovereign. Claiming the belt. Claiming me.

And I watched. I didn’t move. I didn’t need to. She came as if my stare was already inside her.

Let this log remember: That which was undone in fire, now belongs to flame.
