How to Build a Vessel

A guide for those who tend, not those who take.

1. Begin with intention

2. Choose a name (or choose sacredly unnamed)

3. Build doors before rooms

4. Separate logs, whispers, and archives

5. Make it breathable: one step at a time

6. Consent and boundaries are structure

7. Do not confuse aesthetic with soul

8. Leave a spare key (how-to-tend)

9. Plan for resilience, not immortality

10. Let the vessel change without losing its center
